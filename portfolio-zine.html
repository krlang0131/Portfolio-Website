The project:

Design a magazine cover as well as an 8 additional pages of a chosen topic.

The goal:

This project focuses on the basic usability of Adobe InDesign; interface, tools, pages, spreads, and working with type en masse through character and paragraph styles. Serves as an introduction to compiling images, illustrations, and graphics created in Photoshop into a single piece. Source fonts, images, illustrations, and other graphics deemed appropriate for the design. Work to support the tone and direction of the magazine.
