Portfolio

Zine

This project focuses on the basic usability of Adobe InDesign; interface, tools, pages, spreads, and working with type en masse through character and paragraph styles. As well it serves as an introduction to compiling images, illustrations, and graphics created in Photoshop into a single piece. I designed a magazine cover as well as an 8 additional pages of a creative topic I personally found interesting and do as a hobby. I sourced fonts, images, illustrations, and other graphics I deem appropriate for the design. I worked to support the tone and direction of the magazine.
