Smart Harvest LLC

Project Overview

I developed the 2025 U.S. Marketing Plan for Smart Harvest, a farm-to-freezer snack brand known for its Belgian chocolate–covered fruit. My role involved building the brand’s U.S. marketing strategy, managing social media, creating digital ads and content, and designing a presentation deck to guide leadership on brand positioning and campaign execution.

The Marketing Plan

Goals & Challenges

  • Establish Smart Harvest’s presence in the U.S. market.
  • Define a clear and authentic brand identity that connects “fun snacking” with “healthy indulgence.”
  • Build awareness through strategic social content and digital advertising.
  • Differentiate from competitors through storytelling, authenticity, and ethical sourcing.

Brand Positioning & Identity

Tagline: “Smart Snacking, Harvested with Care”

I positioned the brand around healthy indulgence and farm-to-freezer authenticity, emphasizing quality, family appeal, and modern social relevance. These foundations guided every piece of creative and campaign messaging.

Core Brand Pillars:

  • Fun & Trendy
  • Healthy Indulgence
  • Farm-to-Freezer Authenticity
  • Premium Quality

Visual & Verbal Direction

  • Tone: Playful, positive, and authentic
  • Color Palette: Bright fruit tones balanced with warm neutrals
  • Typography: Bold headlines with clean modern fonts
  • Photography Style: Bright product shots, lifestyle moments, and farm authenticity

Content Strategy & Themes

I built a content framework with five recurring themes to drive engagement and brand consistency:

  • 1. Premium & Indulgent – Showcase Belgian chocolate quality.
  • 2. Ethical & Inclusive – Highlight certifications like Fair Trade and Non-GMO.
  • 3. Smart Family Choice – Position as a healthy treat for families.
  • 4. Seasonal & Fun – Align with holidays, trends, and snack culture.
  • 5. Roadshow Engagement – Create weekly posts for retail awareness.

Social Media Management

I managed and curated Smart Harvest’s U.S. social presence, focusing on awareness, engagement, and community connection.

Key Contributions:

  • Designed and scheduled posts aligned with seasonal campaigns.
  • Wrote captions using brand voice guidelines.
  • Engaged with audiences and increase product awareness.

Influencer Collaboration

To expand reach and build authentic connections, I partnered with influencers whose audiences aligned with Smart Harvest’s target market — health-conscious families and lifestyle consumers.

Focus Areas:

  • Identified and vetted micro-influencers focused on healthy eating and family living.
  • Coordinated product shipments, talking points, and campaign briefs.
  • Managed content approvals and reposted influencer content to brand channels.
  • Tracked engagement from influencer partnerships to measure awareness and reach.

Digital Ads & Campaigns

I created paid and organic ad creatives to increase brand reach and awareness.

Focus Areas:

  • Product Giveaways – Executed social media giveaways to boost brand awareness, grow followers, and encourage audience engagement.
  • Roadshow Promotions – Developed digital campaigns to support retail roadshows and drive in-store product sales.
  • Targeted Social Media Advertising – Designed and managed paid campaigns focused on specific U.S. regions and retail store locations, targeting health-conscious consumers to boost visibility and brand recognition.

Marketing Presentation

I designed a professional marketing presentation aimed at securing retail partnerships and convincing major stores to carry Smart Harvest products. The presentation highlighted the brand’s market position, unique farm-to-freezer story, and competitive advantages to help retail buyers visualize consumer demand and shelf appeal.
