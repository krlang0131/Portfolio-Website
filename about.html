Hello, I am Kristina Lang.

 I’m a Pennsylvania-based designer with a background in digital arts and a growing career in UX/UI, branding, and marketing design. My work blends creativity with strategy, focusing on user-centered design that’s both intuitive and visually compelling.

As a freelance designer, I’ve collaborated with small businesses and startups to create responsive websites, social media assets, print materials, and brand visuals. I specialize in wireframing, prototyping, and layout design using tools like Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, and Canva. I also bring foundational front-end knowledge—HTML, CSS, and platforms like WordPress and Webflow—to bridge the gap between design and implementation.

My design process is rooted in empathy, adaptability, and real-world problem solving. I thrive on learning through doing and value honest collaboration, creative exploration, and design that makes an impact. Whether I’m crafting a logo, structuring a blog page, or reimagining a website, I aim to bring clarity, function, and delight to every project.

Outside of design, I’m a parent, a community volunteer, and someone who finds creativity in everyday life—whether I’m baking a new recipe, art projects, or playing games with my family. These roles have taught me the importance of balance, resilience, and curiosity—all of which fuel my work as a designer.
