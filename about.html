Hello, I am Kristina Lang.

 I am a Pennsylvania-based designer with a passion for creating intuitive and visually engaging digital experiences. I hold a Digital Arts degree and a professional certificate in UX/UI Design. As a freelance designer, I specialize in wireframing, prototyping, and interface design, using tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Canva. I have experience integrating web technologies like HTML and CSS to bring designs to life.

My approach to design is rooted in empathy, ensuring that the user’s needs are always at the forefront. I enjoy crafting solutions that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. I am deeply committed to continuous learning, consistently expanding my knowledge in the ever-evolving field of UX/UI design.

Beyond my professional pursuits, my family is a significant part of my life, providing balance and inspiration. In my free time, I enjoy art, baking, and gaming—activities that fuel my creativity and problem-solving skills.

I am excited to bring my skills and passions to innovative design projects and contribute to creating meaningful user experiences. Feel free to connect with me for collaboration, freelance work, or full-time opportunities.
