Research & Industry Analysis

To create a compelling and effective brand identity for this cybersecurity start-up, I conducted extensive research on the industry, exploring the services offered by leading cybersecurity companies and analyzing how they structure and present information. With minimal initial direction from the client, I took the initiative to study industry standards, common service offerings, and best practices in website design and branding for cybersecurity firms.

My research included examining major cybersecurity companies, their service models, visual identities, and user experience strategies. This helped me develop a brand and website structure that aligns with industry expectations while setting the company apart with a unique and modern approach. By understanding the competitive landscape, I was able to craft a strategic and visually cohesive design that effectively communicates professionalism, security, and trust.
