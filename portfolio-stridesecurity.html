Research & Industry Analysis

To create a compelling and effective brand identity for this cybersecurity start-up, I conducted extensive research on the industry, exploring the services offered by leading cybersecurity companies and analyzing how they structure and present information. With minimal initial direction from the client, I took the initiative to study industry standards, common service offerings, and best practices in website design and branding for cybersecurity firms.

My research included examining major cybersecurity companies, their service models, visual identities, and user experience strategies. This helped me develop a brand and website structure that aligns with industry expectations while setting the company apart with a unique and modern approach. By understanding the competitive landscape, I was able to craft a strategic and visually cohesive design that effectively communicates professionalism, security, and trust.

Design Decisions

I explored multiple visual design directions for the site, guided by current trends and industry color associations. After several rounds of client feedback, we decided on a darker color scheme featuring deep blue tones with lighter blue accents. Subtle hints of purple were added to provide contrast and enhance visual interest throughout the design.

Final Design

I continued with the chosen color scheme to maintain consistency throughout the project. I created a total of 11 pages, including a fully functional prototype. The design features 4 pages dedicated to the company's services, where I specifically sourced and customized digital images to align with the topics discussed in each service. I worked closely with the client to determine the essential content needed for the site, ensuring that the design met their objectives and audience needs. Additionally, I incorporated a contact form to facilitate easy communication for potential clients.
