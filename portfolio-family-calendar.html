Family Calendar

Product

A family calendar that manages multiple calendars and allowing multiple people to be apart of a calendar to make events and recieve event updates.


Goal

Design for social good by creating a dedicated mobile app and a complementary responsive website.

Problem

Not having seperate calendars for different parts of your life, that others can also view.

User Research

Persona: Beth

Problem statement: Beth is a busy mom who needs a family calendar because her 3 children have lots of different event each week that her and her family needs to keep track of.

Ideation

Paper Wireframe

Taking the mobile first design approach I sketching the layout for the main feature of the app, the calendar. The calendar layout for the main feature will help dictate the general layout for the rest of the app and additional features. During sketching I needed to consider which buttons where the most important to have access to and include side and bottom menus for additional navigation.

Digital Wireframe

Family Calendar App Wireframe

Mobile Design

In the mobile design I wanted to focus on how the end user might want to view their calendar and how to provide at ease of viewing. I included a collapsible side menu for quick assess to additonal options for their calendar with quick asses to change between calendars.
