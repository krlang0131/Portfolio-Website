Family Calendar

Product

A family calendar designed to streamline the management of multiple schedules, enabling multiple users to collaborate on a shared calendar by creating events and receiving real-time updates.

Goal

Design for social good by creating a dedicated mobile app and a complementary responsive website.

Problem

Not having seperate calendars for different parts of your life, that others can also view.

Role

User Researcher

UX/UI Designer

User Research

Competitive Audit

I conducted a competitive audit of existing companies offering calendar-sharing features. My analysis focused on their functionality, accessibility, navigation, and overall product design. The audit included a detailed review of three platforms: FamilyWall: Family Organizer, Cozi Family Organizer, and Google Calendar.

Persona: Beth

Problem statement: Beth is a busy mom who needs a family calendar because her 3 children have lots of different event each week that her and her family needs to keep track of.

Ideation

Paper Wireframe

Adopting a mobile-first design approach, I sketched the layout for the app's primary feature: the calendar. This design serves as the foundation for the overall app layout and its additional features. While sketching, I carefully considered the placement and accessibility of key buttons and incorporated side and bottom menus to enhance navigation.

Digital Wireframe

Through user research, I identified three core features to develop for the app: Family Calendar, Lists, and Recipes. I then analyzed the functionality of each feature and outlined the necessary options to enhance usability and accessibility. To accommodate the mobile version, I designed a compact navigation system, incorporating a bottom navigation bar for the primary features, a collapsible side menu, and additional top navigation buttons.

Family Calendar App Wireframe

Mobile Design

In the mobile design, I focused on optimizing how users might prefer to view their calendars, ensuring ease and clarity. To enhance functionality, I incorporated a collapsible side menu, providing quick access to additional calendar options and allowing seamless switching between calendars.

Family Calendar Mobile App

Web Design

In the web design, I enhanced the calendar by expanding its size for improved visibility. The previously hidden side menu from the mobile design was transformed into a prominent feature, providing users with easy access to all options and functionalities, thereby improving accessibility. Consistency in the design system was maintained, with a greater emphasis on navigation to optimize the user experience for the larger web interface.

Family Calendar Web Design

Design System

After designing the mobile app version of the family calendar, I developed a design system to establish comprehensive standards and reusable components. This streamlined the process of creating a compatible web version, ensuring consistency while improving efficiency and speed during development.
