Bizarro Branding

The project:

The Bizarro Branding project reassigned an entirely new product to an existing company and re-imagine the branding for that new product.

The goal:

The new product was to be markedly different from the existing product that company currently supplies. Maintain the spirit of the existing company when creating the branding for its new product.

Major milestones:

selection of a brand

reassignment of its supplied product or service

development of a new brand position

conceptualization of new branding collateral

production, editing, and distribution.

Brand: Dunkin'

Brand reasearch:


Existing ads:

I took the existing brand Dunkin' and reimagined this coffee and donut company to now be a shoe company. I created five pieces of branding and advertisments with the revised brand position yet continuing the existing tone of the company's branding. I wanted to keep the existing tag line "America Runs on Dunkin'" and redesign it to a shoe product focus. Dunkin's brand font, coloring, and styling was continued through the redesign.
