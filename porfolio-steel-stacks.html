Steel Stacks


Showcase

A series of photographs capturing the passage of time and its effects on man-made structures. The collection explores a thought-provoking question: what would happen if humanity vanished and nature reclaimed its dominance? It highlights the fragility of human creations in contrast to the enduring power of nature, revealing a unique beauty in the decay of once-functional objects. This series showcases my keen eye for composition and meticulous attention to detail.

Location: Steel Stacks, Bethlehem, PA

Date: November 24th, 2023
