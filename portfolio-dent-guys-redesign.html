The Dent Guy Inc Redesign

Client

The Dent Guys Inc

Proposal

The Dent Guys Inc. is a small business specializing in paintless dent repair services in the Harford County, Maryland area. The client sought a modernized website with updated content and functionality. I was entrusted with full design autonomy to revamp the website. The client additionally requested advertisements design for social media to enhance their business visibility and promote their services via social media.

Role

UX/UI Designer, Web Developer

Tools

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Figma
  • Canva
  • Github

Redesign

Before

After

The original design was outdated, with an overcrowded layout that made the content difficult to navigate. The design was restructured by organizing the information more effectively, emphasizing their work, services, and client reviews. As part of the redesign, a total of nine pages were created, including additional pages to provide a more detailed presentation of their services. I now serve as the webmaster for their small business, managing updates and maintaining their website as needed.
