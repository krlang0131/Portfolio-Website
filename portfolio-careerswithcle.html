Careers with Cliff Lewis Experience

Project Overview

Created a professional, conversion-focused recruitment website for Careers with CLE to help attract ambitious real estate agents. The site highlights the team’s collaborative culture, proven mentorship programs, structured support systems, and impressive track record ($800M+ in sales and 2,500+ homes sold in the last 5 years)

Client

Cliff Lewis Experience

Role

Digital Designer, Web Designer, Web Developer

Tools

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • WordPress
  • Github

Objectives

  • Communicate a supportive yet growth-oriented team environment (“Laid back in culture. Serious about growth.”)
  • Make it easy for agents at different experience levels to see themselves succeeding with CLE
  • Drive applications through clear calls-to-action

Design & Development

  • Built a clean, modern, responsive website using WordPress
  • Created structured sections including team growth formula, mentorship benefits, lead systems, and success metrics
  • Used motivational yet professional visuals and typography to reflect both approachability and serious business results
  • Optimized for mobile since many agents browse opportunities on their phones

Key Features

  • Compelling hero section with strong tagline: “Inspired by People. Powered by Experience.”
  • Clear breakdown of the “Proven Formula” (leadership, production engine, diverse leads, national scale)
  • Targeted messaging for newly licensed, plateaued, and experienced agents
  • Prominent “Join Our Team” CTA linking to the application page

Results

The live website effectively showcases CLE’s unique value and supports ongoing recruitment efforts for team expansion.
