Art Auction App

The product:

An art auction app for users interested in bidding on art pieces from art galleries


Project duration:

May 2023 - October 2023

Project overview

The problem:

Not knowing when art pieces go live for auction or if others outbid on pieces you are interested in.

The goal:

Create an app that allows customizable notifications for users to recieve desired art auction info.

Design thinking process:

Understanding the user

Conducted fictional interviews and created empthy maps to understand the users I'm designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through research was working dults who don't have time to constantly chek their apps for updates.

User research: pain points

Persona: Dave

Problem statement

Dave is a busy working adult who needs to be notified if art he is interested in has been outbid or auction is coming to an end because he is too busy to check daily
